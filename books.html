Herbal Rhymes: A Mnemonic Study Guide for TCM Herbology

Second Edition · By Dr. Stacy Peck, DACM, L.Ac.

Stop memorizing. Start remembering. This book transforms 217 TCM herbs from an overwhelming data dump into memorable, clinically relevant verse. Each poem is engineered to encode the properties you'll be tested on — temperature, taste, channels, actions, and contraindications — so you can recall them under exam pressure.

Built on mnemonic science and verified against Bensky's Materia Medica, 3rd Edition, Herbal Rhymes is the study companion that works the way your brain actually learns: through pattern, story, and rhyme.

  • 217 herbs from Bensky's Materia Medica, 3rd Edition
  • 70+ original mnemonic poems encoding herb properties
  • Board Pearls highlighting must-know exam facts
  • Memory tricks and clinical association cues
  • Comparison tables for commonly confused herb pairs
  • 150+ practice exam questions with detailed answer explanations
  • Color-coded herb categories for visual organization
  • Covers content for both NCCAOM and CALE exams
